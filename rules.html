Game Rules

Read the full list of rules to make sure you know how to play fairly!

  • Respect other players! Treat everyone fairly and kindly.
  • No cheating or exploiting glitches in the game.
  • Stay active and engaged in the game, no AFK players.
  • No abusive language or behavior in the game or community.
  • Follow all in-game instructions and server rules.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid any harmful behavior.
  • Work together with your team to tag others, don't abandon teammates.
  • If you're tagged as "it," give others a chance to escape before you tag them.
  • Follow the guidelines for fair play and ensure an enjoyable experience for all players.
  • Report any issues or bugs to the moderators or game admins.
