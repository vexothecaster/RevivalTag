How to Play Revival Tag

Follow these steps to play the game and have a great time!

  • Move like a gorilla: use your hands to run around!
  • Avoid being tagged by the "it" player.
  • Work together with your team to tag others.
  • If you're tagged by the "it" player, you become "it"!
  • The goal is to avoid getting tagged and tag other players in the game.
  • Make sure to communicate with your team to strategize and win!
  • Use the environment to your advantage, like climbing trees and obstacles.
